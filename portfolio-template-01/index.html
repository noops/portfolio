Hi, I'm Ben!

Data Analyst - Big Data - Story Telling

Data analyst with an applied math degree from Southern Methodist University and a certification in data visualization from the University of Texas. Passionate about dynamical systems and chaos theory. Well versed in python, git, SQL, flask, and mongoDB. Enjoy using dataframes to organize and display data via easy to read visualizations. Developed strong leadership and interpersonal skills after living and working as a ski instructor for two winters. These interpersonal skills combined with my analytical skills make me a great candidate for any team.
